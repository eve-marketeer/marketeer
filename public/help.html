Marketeer

Version 0.1.0

A market companion for EVE Online that watches your exports and copies competitive prices to your clipboard.

How to Use

1 Open the Market window in EVE Online and search for an item
2 Click the Export to File button in the market window menu
3 Marketeer automatically detects the export and copies the adjusted price
4 Paste directly into EVE's price field
